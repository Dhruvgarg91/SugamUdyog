Healthcare schemes

The PMJAY is a government health insurance scheme for individuals belonging to the lower socio-economic strata of society. Under this scheme, each household can get medical coverage of up to ₹5 Lakh for secondary and tertiary hospitalisation care. Furthermore, the government health insurance scheme has no restriction on age, family size or gender for beneficiaries.

Awaz Health Insurance Scheme

The Awaz Health Insurance Scheme was launched by the Government of Kerala in 2017 with the intention of providing health insurance to inter-state labourers spread across the state. In addition to providing them with financial assistance in the case of medical emergencies, the scheme also offers an accidental death benefit to the beneficiary’s nominees. A policyholder can claim medical expenses of up to ₹15,000, whereas the death benefit amounts to ₹2 Lakh, which is applicable to all labourers between the ages of 18 and 60.
